Our Services

Photography

A moment can be captured in a myriad of ways, but not every snapshot conveys the same message. At Studio Chandan, we work hard to maintain the emotion and genuineness of each shot, creating intentional, distraction-free photos that capture real moments that will last a lifetime.

Our Photography Options:

  • Passport Size Photos (3.5 × 4.5 cm)
  • Wall Frames (Various sizes from 12×18" to 24×36")
  • A4 Size Prints (8.27 × 11.69 inches)
  • Poster Card Size (11 × 17 inches)
  • Custom-sized Photo Albums
Aerial Photography

Aerial photography provides a completely different viewpoint. Our drone-powered photos show breathtaking details from above, whether you're covering events, real estate, or expansive landscapes.

Aerial Photography Options:

  • Real Estate Aerial Coverage
  • Event Aerial Documentation
  • Landscape & Nature Panoramas
  • Construction Progress Tracking
  • Architectural & Property Showcasing
Videography

Our videography captures the true spirit, passion, and emotion of each moment. Our goal is to produce genuine, self-evident cinematic experiences that tell your story authentically.

Videography Services:

  • Wedding & Event Cinematography
  • Corporate Video Production
  • Music & Promotional Videos
  • Documentary & Short Films
  • 4K Video Resolution Options
Digital Frames

Your memories come to life in a sleek, contemporary manner with digital frames, allowing you to easily and beautifully display a revolving collection of your most treasured experiences.

Digital Frame Options:

Audio Visual System
